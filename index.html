About Thai-Takeaway

Situated what looks like a hole in the wall this elegent take-away is nested in between the greatist street art that Helsinki has to offer.

Having your favourate Thai foods in variety of flavours to cater to vegetarians,vegens and meatlovers alike.

"It's never too early, nor too late to eat Thai-food" Fresh is the new sweet.

Opening hours: weekdays from 11am to 7pm. Weekend closed

Address:Ratamestarinkatu 3 Heslinki, Finland
